# Business Expansion Analysis & Marketing ROI

A data-driven analysis project focused on evaluating the success of business expansion and marketing efficiency across different regions.

##Project Overview
The goal of this project is to analyze the performance of various store locations. By calculating the Return on Marketing Spend (ROMS) and analyzing revenue/profit across regions, this study identifies which areas are most suitable for future growth.

##  Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python
* **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn, Matplotlib.
* **Key Metrics Analyzed:** Revenue, Profit, Marketing Spend, ROMS (Return on Marketing Spend).

## Key Insights
- **Marketing Efficiency:** Calculated ROMS to determine the effectiveness of marketing campaigns in driving revenue.
- **Regional Performance:** Compared Region 1 and Region 2 to identify top-performing territories.
- **New vs. Old:** Analyzed the profitability of newly expanded stores compared to established ones.
- **Visualization:** Utilized Boxplots to understand profit distribution and identify outliers in store performance.

## Results
- Identified the top 10 states by store count and profitability.
- Provided clear visualizations showing the correlation between marketing spend and revenue growth.
